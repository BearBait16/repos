Overview

Purpose

I would like to share information on my favorite subject. Dungeouns and Dragons. This will be some sort of information hub for players.

Audience

My main audience is my friends who play DND with me. They are constantly asking me about rules and how to apply them in game. In the game of Dungeouns and Dragons there are 3 full length textbooks that give you the basics. In other words, that's a lot of rules. This will not only help them, but any other struggling DM who is constatnly needing to pause the game to explain the rules. This will be a quick reference for them to study in between, or to use in off time while playing. They will often use their tablet or their laptop.
